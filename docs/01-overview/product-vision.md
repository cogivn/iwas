# Product Vision

**Product:** IWAS – iCafe WiFi Access Service  
**Last Updated:** February 16, 2026

---

## Vision Statement

Create a **seamless WiFi monetization platform** that integrates with existing iCafe infrastructure, enabling customers to extend their gaming experience to personal devices while generating incremental revenue without impacting core PC gaming performance.

---

## Mission

Empower iCafe businesses to maximize revenue from existing network infrastructure while delivering exceptional WiFi experiences to customers through:

- **Seamless Integration** - Works with existing PC management systems
- **Flexible Payments** - Multiple payment methods for maximum convenience
- **Zero Impact** - No degradation to PC gaming performance
- **Easy Management** - Centralized control across all locations
- **Fair Usage** - Anti-abuse mechanisms to ensure quality for all users

---

## Problem Statement

### Current Situation

**Customer Pain Points:**

- iCafe customers want WiFi access for personal devices while using PCs
- No convenient way to pay for WiFi using existing PC account
- Complex login procedures create friction
- Expensive standalone WiFi packages

**Business Pain Points:**

- Existing network infrastructure is underutilized for WiFi services
- No integrated billing solution between PC rental and WiFi access
- Manual WiFi management creates operational overhead
- Limited revenue streams beyond PC rental
- Difficulty tracking WiFi usage and revenue

**Technical Challenges:**

- No network segmentation between PC gaming and WiFi traffic
- Risk of WiFi traffic impacting gaming performance
- Lack of device binding and session management
- No centralized management for multiple locations

### Desired Outcome

**For Customers:**

- ✅ One-click WiFi access with Google login
- ✅ Link payment methods once, use everywhere
- ✅ Affordable, flexible WiFi packages
- ✅ Reliable connection quality
- ✅ Works across all iCafe locations

**For Business:**

- ✅ Automated WiFi service with integrated billing
- ✅ Additional revenue stream with minimal investment
- ✅ Zero impact on PC gaming network performance
- ✅ Scalable solution across multiple locations
- ✅ Real-time monitoring and analytics

**For Operations:**

- ✅ Centralized management dashboard
- ✅ Automated billing and session management
- ✅ Fraud prevention and anti-abuse controls
- ✅ Minimal technical maintenance required

## Core Value Propositions

### 1. Seamless User Experience

**Problem:** Complex authentication and payment processes create friction  
**Solution:** Google OAuth + Payment Wallet for one-click purchases

**Benefits:**

- Login once with Google, use everywhere
- Link payment methods once, use forever
- 1-click purchase on return visits
- Cross-location support

### 2. Revenue Maximization

**Problem:** Underutilized network infrastructure  
**Solution:** Monetize existing WiFi capacity with minimal investment

**Benefits:**

- New revenue stream (5-10M VND per location/month)
- 30% adoption rate target
- Upsell opportunities for PC customers
- Automated billing reduces overhead

### 3. Quality Assurance

**Problem:** WiFi traffic might impact PC gaming performance  
**Solution:** VLAN isolation + QoS bandwidth management

**Benefits:**

- Zero impact on PC gaming traffic
- Guaranteed bandwidth for WiFi users
- Low latency for gaming applications
- Fair usage enforcement

### 4. Operational Efficiency

**Problem:** Manual WiFi management is time-consuming  
**Solution:** Centralized dashboard with automation

**Benefits:**

- Real-time monitoring across all locations
- Automated session management
- Revenue reporting and analytics
- Minimal manual intervention

### 5. Fraud Prevention

**Problem:** WiFi sharing and abuse reduce profitability  
**Solution:** Device binding + session limits + anti-abuse controls

**Benefits:**

- One device per session
- Concurrent session limits
- Hotspot/tethering detection
- Fair usage policies

## Competitive Advantages

### 1. Integrated Billing

Unlike standalone WiFi services, IWAS integrates directly with PC account systems for seamless payments.

### 2. Payment Flexibility

Support for PC balance, e-wallets (Momo, ZaloPay, VNPay), and bank QR codes - more options than competitors.

### 3. Cross-Location Support

Single account works across all iCafe locations - unique in the market.

### 4. Zero Gaming Impact

VLAN isolation ensures WiFi traffic never affects PC gaming performance.

### 5. Advanced Anti-Abuse

Device binding and session management prevent sharing and abuse.
