# Target Market

**Product:** IWAS – iCafe WiFi Access Service  
**Last Updated:** February 16, 2026

---

## Market Overview

### Primary Market

**iCafe Locations with 20-100 PC Stations**

- **Size:** 500+ locations in Vietnam
- **Characteristics:**
  - Established PC rental business
  - Existing network infrastructure
  - Customer base of regular gamers
  - Located in urban and suburban areas
  - Average 50-80 PCs per location
- **Pain Points:**
  - Limited revenue streams beyond PC rental
  - Underutilized network capacity
  - Customer requests for WiFi access
  - Manual WiFi management overhead
  - Difficulty tracking WiFi usage

- **Opportunity:**
  - 30% of PC customers want WiFi access
  - Average 5-10M VND additional monthly revenue per location
  - Minimal additional infrastructure investment
  - Leverage existing PC account system

### Secondary Market

**Gaming Centers and Internet Cafes**

- **Size:** 200+ locations in Vietnam
- **Characteristics:**
  - Smaller operations (10-30 PCs)
  - Mixed PC rental and gaming events
  - Tech-savvy customer base
  - Urban locations
- **Opportunity:**
  - Similar WiFi demand from customers
  - Potential for event-based WiFi packages
  - Cross-selling opportunities

## Geographic Focus

### Initial Launch: Vietnam

**Why Vietnam?**

1. **Large iCafe Market**
   - 700+ iCafe locations nationwide
   - Growing gaming culture
   - High smartphone penetration (>80%)

2. **Payment Infrastructure**
   - Mature e-wallet ecosystem (Momo, ZaloPay, VNPay)
   - VietQR standard for bank transfers
   - High mobile payment adoption

3. **Market Readiness**
   - Customers expect WiFi availability
   - Willingness to pay for quality service
   - Existing PC account systems for integration

**Target Cities (Phase 1):**

- **Ho Chi Minh City** - 200+ iCafes
- **Hanoi** - 150+ iCafes
- **Da Nang** - 50+ iCafes

**Expansion Plan:**

- **Month 1-3:** Pilot in 3 locations (HCMC)
- **Month 4-6:** Expand to 10 locations (HCMC, Hanoi)
- **Month 7-12:** Scale to 50+ locations (major cities)

## Customer Segments

### Segment 1: Regular PC Gamers (Primary)

**Profile:**

- Age: 16-30 years old
- Frequency: 3-5 visits per week
- Session duration: 2-6 hours
- PC account balance: 50,000-200,000 VND
- Owns smartphone and/or laptop

**Needs:**

- WiFi for personal devices during PC sessions
- Quick, convenient authentication
- Affordable pricing
- Reliable connection

**WiFi Use Cases:**

- Social media browsing
- Messaging apps (Zalo, Messenger)
- Streaming music/videos
- Mobile gaming during breaks
- Work/study on laptop

**Willingness to Pay:** 5,000-10,000 VND per session

**Adoption Potential:** 30-40% of PC customers

### Segment 2: Casual PC Users (Secondary)

**Profile:**

- Age: 18-35 years old
- Frequency: 1-2 visits per week
- Session duration: 1-3 hours
- PC account balance: 20,000-100,000 VND
- Owns smartphone

**Needs:**

- Occasional WiFi access
- Simple payment process
- Flexible packages (hourly)

**WiFi Use Cases:**

- Work on laptop while using PC
- Video calls
- File downloads

**Willingness to Pay:** 3,000-7,000 VND per session

**Adoption Potential:** 20-30% of PC customers

### Segment 3: Guest Users (Tertiary)

**Profile:**

- Age: 18-40 years old
- No PC account
- Visiting iCafe for WiFi only
- Owns smartphone/laptop

**Needs:**

- WiFi access without PC rental
- Easy authentication (Google)
- Flexible payment (e-wallet, bank QR)

**WiFi Use Cases:**

- Remote work
- Study sessions
- Meetings
- General browsing

**Willingness to Pay:** 10,000-20,000 VND per session

**Adoption Potential:** 10-15% of WiFi users

## Market Size & Opportunity

### Total Addressable Market (TAM)

**Vietnam iCafe Market:**

- 700+ iCafe locations
- Average 60 PCs per location
- Average 200 customers per location per day
- 30% WiFi adoption rate = 60 WiFi customers per location per day

**Revenue Potential:**

- Average WiFi spend: 7,000 VND per customer
- Daily revenue per location: 420,000 VND
- Monthly revenue per location: 12.6M VND
- **Total TAM: 8.8B VND per month (700 locations)**

### Serviceable Addressable Market (SAM)

**Target: iCafes with 20-100 PCs**

- 500 locations
- **SAM: 6.3B VND per month**

### Serviceable Obtainable Market (SOM)

**Year 1 Target: 50 locations**

- **SOM: 630M VND per month**
- **Annual: 7.5B VND**

## Competitive Landscape

### Direct Competitors

**1. Standalone WiFi Services**

- **Strengths:** Established, simple
- **Weaknesses:** No PC integration, separate payment, limited features
- **Our Advantage:** Integrated billing, cross-location support

**2. Free iCafe WiFi**

- **Strengths:** Free for customers
- **Weaknesses:** No revenue, no quality control, abuse issues
- **Our Advantage:** Monetization, quality assurance, anti-abuse

### Indirect Competitors

**1. Mobile Data Plans**

- **Strengths:** Always available, no login
- **Weaknesses:** Limited data, expensive, slow indoor speeds
- **Our Advantage:** Unlimited data, faster speeds, cheaper

**2. Coffee Shop WiFi**

- **Strengths:** Free with purchase
- **Weaknesses:** Not gaming-focused, crowded, unreliable
- **Our Advantage:** Gaming environment, reliable, device-friendly

## Market Trends

### 1. Growing Gaming Culture

- Esports popularity increasing
- PC gaming remains dominant in Vietnam
- Mobile gaming complementing PC gaming

### 2. Multi-Device Usage

- Customers bring multiple devices (phone, laptop, tablet)
- Demand for simultaneous connectivity
- Work-from-anywhere culture

### 3. Payment Digitalization

- E-wallet adoption growing rapidly
- QR code payments becoming standard
- Reduced cash transactions

### 4. Quality Expectations

- Customers expect fast, reliable WiFi
- Willingness to pay for quality
- Intolerance for slow/unreliable connections

## Go-to-Market Strategy

### Phase 1: Pilot (Month 1-3)

**Target:** 3 locations in HCMC

- Select high-traffic iCafes
- Diverse customer demographics
- Willing to provide feedback

**Goals:**

- Validate product-market fit
- Test pricing and packages
- Gather user feedback
- Optimize operations

### Phase 2: Expansion (Month 4-6)

**Target:** 10 locations (HCMC, Hanoi)

- Proven iCafe partners
- High customer volume
- Good network infrastructure

**Goals:**

- Achieve 30% adoption rate
- Validate revenue targets
- Refine features based on feedback
- Build case studies

### Phase 3: Scale (Month 7-12)

**Target:** 50+ locations (major cities)

- Expand to Da Nang, Can Tho, Hai Phong
- Partner with iCafe chains
- Franchise opportunities

**Goals:**

- Achieve market leadership
- Build brand recognition
- Optimize unit economics
- Prepare for regional expansion

## Market Entry Barriers

### Low Barriers

✅ Existing iCafe infrastructure  
✅ Established customer base  
✅ Mature payment ecosystem  
✅ High smartphone penetration

### Moderate Barriers

⚠️ Integration with PC systems (varies by vendor)  
⚠️ Network configuration requirements  
⚠️ Staff training needed

### Mitigation Strategies

- Partner with PC system vendors
- Provide turnkey installation
- Offer comprehensive training
- 24/7 technical support
