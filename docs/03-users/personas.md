# User Personas

**Product:** IWAS – iCafe WiFi Access Service  
**Last Updated:** February 16, 2026

---

## Overview

This document defines the key user personas for IWAS, including their profiles, needs, pain points, and behaviors. Understanding these personas is critical for designing features and user experiences that meet real user needs.

---

## Primary Personas

### 1. PC Customer (Primary User)

**Profile:**

- **Age:** 16-30 years old
- **Occupation:** Students, young professionals, gamers
- **Tech Savviness:** Medium to high
- **Income:** 5-15M VND/month
- **Location:** Urban and suburban areas
- **Device Ownership:** Smartphone (100%), laptop (40%)

**iCafe Behavior:**

- **Frequency:** 3-5 visits per week
- **Session Duration:** 2-6 hours per visit
- **PC Account Balance:** 50,000-200,000 VND
- **Primary Activity:** Gaming (LOL, PUBG, Valorant, etc.)
- **Secondary Activity:** Social media, streaming, work/study

**Needs:**

1. **WiFi Access for Personal Devices**
   - Use smartphone during PC gaming breaks
   - Work on laptop while using PC
   - Stream music/videos on personal device

2. **Simple Authentication**
   - No complex login procedures
   - Quick access without creating new accounts
   - Remember me across visits

3. **Affordable Pricing**
   - Reasonable cost (5,000-10,000 VND per session)
   - Flexible package options
   - Value for money

4. **Reliable Connection**
   - Fast speeds (10-20 Mbps)
   - Stable connection
   - No interruptions during use

5. **Convenience**
   - Pay with existing PC balance
   - One-click purchase on return visits
   - Works across all iCafe locations

**Pain Points:**

1. **No WiFi Available**
   - Many iCafes don't offer WiFi
   - Or WiFi is unreliable/slow

2. **Complex Login Procedures**
   - Multiple steps to connect
   - Need to re-login every visit
   - Different credentials for each location

3. **Expensive Standalone WiFi**
   - Separate payment required
   - No integration with PC account
   - Higher prices than mobile data

4. **Connection Issues**
   - Frequent disconnections
   - Slow speeds
   - No support when issues occur

5. **Limited Payment Options**
   - Cash only
   - No e-wallet support
   - Can't use PC balance

**Goals:**

- ✅ Stay connected on personal devices during PC sessions
- ✅ Minimize friction in authentication and payment
- ✅ Get reliable, fast WiFi at affordable prices
- ✅ Use the same account across all iCafe locations

**Motivations:**

- **Convenience:** Want seamless experience
- **Value:** Want good quality at fair price
- **Flexibility:** Want multiple payment options
- **Consistency:** Want same experience everywhere

**Frustrations:**

- Having to use mobile data (expensive, limited)
- Complex login processes
- Unreliable free WiFi
- Different systems at each location

**Quote:**

> "I just want to connect my phone quickly and pay with my PC balance. Why is it so complicated?"

### 2. Guest User (Secondary User)

**Profile:**

- **Age:** 18-40 years old
- **Occupation:** Remote workers, students, freelancers
- **Tech Savviness:** Medium to high
- **Income:** 8-20M VND/month
- **Location:** Urban areas
- **Device Ownership:** Smartphone (100%), laptop (80%)

**iCafe Behavior:**

- **Frequency:** 1-3 visits per month
- **Session Duration:** 2-4 hours per visit
- **PC Account:** No PC account (WiFi only)
- **Primary Activity:** Work, study, meetings
- **Secondary Activity:** Browsing, streaming

**Needs:**

1. **WiFi Without PC Rental**
   - Access WiFi without renting a PC
   - Use iCafe as co-working space
   - Quiet environment for work/study

2. **Easy Authentication**
   - Login with Google (no new account)
   - Quick access
   - Secure authentication

3. **Flexible Payment**
   - E-wallet (Momo, ZaloPay, VNPay)
   - Bank QR code
   - No cash required

4. **Quality Connection**
   - Fast, reliable WiFi
   - Suitable for video calls
   - Stable for work

**Pain Points:**

1. **Can't Access WiFi Without PC**
   - Many iCafes require PC rental
   - Expensive to rent PC just for WiFi

2. **No Guest Access**
   - Need to create PC account
   - Complex registration process

3. **Limited Payment Options**
   - Cash only
   - No e-wallet support

4. **Unreliable WiFi**
   - Slow speeds
   - Frequent disconnections
   - No quality guarantee

**Goals:**

- ✅ Access quality WiFi without PC rental
- ✅ Quick, easy authentication
- ✅ Pay with preferred method (e-wallet, bank)
- ✅ Reliable connection for work/study

**Motivations:**

- **Productivity:** Need reliable WiFi for work
- **Convenience:** Want easy access
- **Flexibility:** Want to pay with e-wallet
- **Quality:** Willing to pay for good service

**Quote:**

> "I don't need a PC, just good WiFi to work on my laptop. Let me pay with Momo and get started."

## Administrative Personas

### 3. iCafe Owner/Manager (Key Stakeholder)

**Profile:**

- **Age:** 25-45 years old
- **Occupation:** Business owner/manager
- **Tech Savviness:** Medium
- **Locations Managed:** 1-5 iCafes
- **Team Size:** 2-10 staff members

**Business Context:**

- **Revenue Focus:** Maximize revenue per customer
- **Operational Focus:** Minimize overhead and complexity
- **Customer Focus:** High satisfaction, repeat business
- **Growth Focus:** Scale to multiple locations

**Needs:**

1. **Additional Revenue Stream**
   - Monetize existing WiFi infrastructure
   - Increase revenue per customer
   - Predictable, recurring revenue

2. **Easy Management**
   - Simple dashboard
   - Real-time monitoring
   - Minimal training required

3. **Revenue Insights**
   - Daily/weekly/monthly reports
   - Revenue by location
   - Customer analytics

4. **Fraud Prevention**
   - Detect and prevent abuse
   - Device binding
   - Usage monitoring

5. **Minimal Maintenance**
   - Automated processes
   - Reliable system
   - Quick support when needed

**Pain Points:**

1. **Limited Revenue Streams**
   - Only PC rental revenue
   - Underutilized WiFi infrastructure

2. **Complex Network Management**
   - Technical knowledge required
   - Time-consuming setup
   - Difficult to troubleshoot

3. **No Visibility**
   - Can't track WiFi usage
   - No revenue reporting
   - No customer insights

4. **Customer Complaints**
   - Slow WiFi
   - Connection issues
   - No support

**Goals:**

- ✅ Generate 5-10M VND additional revenue per location
- ✅ Manage all locations from single dashboard
- ✅ Minimize operational overhead
- ✅ Improve customer satisfaction

**Motivations:**

- **Revenue:** Maximize profitability
- **Efficiency:** Minimize time spent on management
- **Growth:** Scale to more locations
- **Quality:** Maintain high service standards

**Quote:**

> "I want to make more money from WiFi without spending all day managing it. Show me the numbers and let it run itself."

### 4. Location Operator (Staff)

**Profile:**

- **Age:** 18-30 years old
- **Occupation:** iCafe staff
- **Tech Savviness:** Low to medium
- **Shift:** 8-hour shifts
- **Responsibilities:** Customer support, basic troubleshooting

**Work Context:**

- **Primary Role:** Help customers with PC and WiFi
- **Secondary Role:** Monitor sessions, handle complaints
- **Tools:** Admin dashboard (view-only)
- **Support:** Escalate complex issues

**Needs:**

1. **Simple Interface**
   - Easy to understand
   - Clear instructions
   - Minimal training

2. **Monitoring Tools**
   - See active sessions
   - Check user status
   - View basic stats

3. **Basic Actions**
   - Force disconnect sessions
   - Help customers connect
   - Check transaction status

4. **Support Resources**
   - FAQ documentation
   - Troubleshooting guides
   - Escalation process

**Pain Points:**

1. **Complex Systems**
   - Too many steps
   - Technical jargon
   - Difficult to learn

2. **Customer Complaints**
   - Connection issues
   - Payment failures
   - Don't know how to help

3. **No Training**
   - Learn on the job
   - No documentation
   - No support

**Goals:**

- ✅ Help customers quickly and effectively
- ✅ Minimize customer complaints
- ✅ Understand system basics
- ✅ Know when to escalate

**Motivations:**

- **Customer Service:** Want to help customers
- **Efficiency:** Want to resolve issues quickly
- **Simplicity:** Want easy-to-use tools
- **Support:** Want backup when stuck

**Quote:**

> "I just need to know how to help customers connect and what to do when there's a problem."

### 5. Super Admin (Technical)

**Profile:**

- **Age:** 25-40 years old
- **Occupation:** System administrator, DevOps
- **Tech Savviness:** High
- **Scope:** All locations
- **Responsibilities:** System configuration, user management, troubleshooting

**Work Context:**

- **Primary Role:** System administration
- **Secondary Role:** Technical support
- **Tools:** Full admin dashboard
- **Authority:** Complete system access

**Needs:**

1. **Full System Control**
   - Manage all locations
   - Configure system settings
   - Create/delete admin accounts

2. **Advanced Monitoring**
   - System health metrics
   - Performance analytics
   - Error logs

3. **User Management**
   - Assign roles and permissions
   - Manage location access
   - Audit user actions

4. **Troubleshooting Tools**
   - View detailed logs
   - Debug issues
   - Remote access

**Goals:**

- ✅ Maintain 99.9% system uptime
- ✅ Quickly resolve technical issues
- ✅ Optimize system performance
- ✅ Ensure security and compliance

**Motivations:**

- **Reliability:** Keep system running smoothly
- **Performance:** Optimize for speed and efficiency
- **Security:** Protect data and prevent breaches
- **Scalability:** Support growth

**Quote:**

> "I need complete visibility and control to keep the system running perfectly across all locations."

## User Segmentation

### By Value

**High-Value Users:**

- Regular PC customers (3-5 visits/week)
- High ARPU (>80,000 VND/month)
- Long session duration (4-6 hours)
- **Strategy:** Loyalty programs, premium features

**Medium-Value Users:**

- Casual PC customers (1-2 visits/week)
- Medium ARPU (30,000-80,000 VND/month)
- Medium session duration (2-4 hours)
- **Strategy:** Upsell to regular, bundle deals

**Low-Value Users:**

- Occasional guests (<1 visit/week)
- Low ARPU (<30,000 VND/month)
- Short session duration (1-2 hours)
- **Strategy:** Acquisition campaigns, trial offers

### By Behavior

**Power Users:**

- Daily visits
- Long sessions
- High spend
- **Strategy:** VIP treatment, exclusive features

**Regular Users:**

- 3-5 visits/week
- Standard sessions
- Consistent spend
- **Strategy:** Retention, loyalty rewards

**Casual Users:**

- 1-2 visits/week
- Variable sessions
- Occasional spend
- **Strategy:** Engagement, upsell

**Trial Users:**

- First-time users
- Short sessions
- Low spend
- **Strategy:** Onboarding, conversion
