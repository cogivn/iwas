# Revenue Model

**Product:** IWAS – iCafe WiFi Access Service  
**Last Updated:** February 16, 2026

---

## Overview

This document details the revenue model for IWAS, including pricing strategy, revenue streams, unit economics, and financial projections.

---

## Pricing Strategy

### Package Pricing

**Time-Based Packages:**

| Package  | Duration | Price      | Price/Hour | Target Segment |
| -------- | -------- | ---------- | ---------- | -------------- |
| Basic    | 1 hour   | 5,000 VND  | 5,000 VND  | Casual users   |
| Standard | 3 hours  | 12,000 VND | 4,000 VND  | Regular users  |
| Premium  | 6 hours  | 20,000 VND | 3,333 VND  | Power users    |

**Pricing Rationale:**

- ✅ Volume discount encourages longer sessions
- ✅ Competitive with mobile data (200-300 VND/GB)
- ✅ Affordable for target demographic
- ✅ Profitable with 60-70% gross margin

### Bandwidth Tiers (Future)

| Tier     | Speed   | Price Premium | Use Case                      |
| -------- | ------- | ------------- | ----------------------------- |
| Standard | 10 Mbps | Base price    | Browsing, social media        |
| Fast     | 20 Mbps | +30%          | Streaming, downloads          |
| Ultra    | 50 Mbps | +50%          | Heavy downloads, 4K streaming |

## Revenue Streams

### 1. WiFi Package Sales (Primary)

**Current:** 100% of revenue

**Projected Breakdown:**

- 1-hour packages: 30-40%
- 3-hour packages: 40-50%
- 6-hour packages: 10-20%

**Monthly Revenue per Location:**

- Month 1-3: 3-5M VND
- Month 4-6: 5-10M VND
- Month 7-12: 10-15M VND

### 2. Transaction Fees (Future - Phase 3)

**Model:** Small fee on e-wallet/bank transactions

**Fee Structure:**

- E-Wallet: 1-2% per transaction
- Bank QR: 500-1,000 VND per transaction
- PC Balance: No fee (to encourage adoption)

**Projected Revenue:** 5-10% of total revenue

### 3. Premium Features (Future - Phase 3)

**Features:**

- Priority bandwidth: +2,000 VND/session
- Multi-device support: +3,000 VND/session
- Extended hours: +1,000 VND/hour beyond package

**Projected Revenue:** 10-15% of total revenue

### 4. B2B Licensing (Future)

**Model:** License IWAS platform to other iCafe chains

**Pricing:**

- Setup fee: 50M VND per chain
- Monthly license: 500K VND per location
- Revenue share: 10% of WiFi revenue

**Projected Revenue:** Significant upside (Year 2+)

## Revenue Optimization Strategies

### 1. Dynamic Pricing

**Strategy:** Adjust prices based on demand

**Implementation:**

- Peak hours (6pm-11pm): +20% premium
- Off-peak hours (9am-5pm): -20% discount
- Weekend pricing: +10% premium

**Expected Impact:** +15-20% revenue

### 2. Package Bundling

**Strategy:** Combine PC rental + WiFi

**Bundles:**

- PC 3h + WiFi 3h: 50,000 VND (save 5,000 VND)
- PC 6h + WiFi 6h: 90,000 VND (save 10,000 VND)

**Expected Impact:** +10-15% adoption, +20% ARPU

### 3. Loyalty Programs

**Strategy:** Reward frequent users

**Tiers:**

- Bronze (5+ purchases): 5% discount
- Silver (10+ purchases): 10% discount
- Gold (20+ purchases): 15% discount + priority support

**Expected Impact:** +25% retention, +30% LTV

### 4. Referral Program

**Strategy:** Incentivize customer referrals

**Rewards:**

- Referrer: 5,000 VND credit
- Referee: 3,000 VND discount on first purchase

**Expected Impact:** 20% of new customers from referrals

### 5. Promotional Campaigns

**Launch Promotion (Month 1):**

- First purchase: 50% off
- Expected cost: 2M VND
- Expected new users: 500
- CAC: 4,000 VND

**Monthly Promotions:**

- Flash sales (limited time)
- Holiday specials
- Student discounts

**Expected Impact:** +10-15% revenue during campaigns

## Revenue Attribution

### By Payment Method

**Target Distribution:**

- PC Balance: 50-60% (lowest friction)
- E-Wallet: 30-40% (guest users)
- Bank QR: 10-20% (occasional users)

**Strategy:**

- Promote PC balance for PC users
- Highlight e-wallet for guest users
- Bank QR as fallback option

### By Customer Segment

**Target Distribution:**

- Regular PC Gamers: 60-70%
- Casual PC Users: 20-30%
- Guest Users: 10-20%

**Strategy:**

- Focus on PC gamers (highest LTV)
- Upsell casual users to regular
- Grow guest user segment

### By Package Type

**Target Distribution:**

- 1-hour: 30-40% (trial users)
- 3-hour: 40-50% (sweet spot)
- 6-hour: 10-20% (power users)

**Strategy:**

- Promote 3-hour as best value
- Upsell 1-hour to 3-hour
- Premium pricing for 6-hour

## Revenue Risks & Mitigation

### Risk 1: Lower Than Expected Adoption

**Impact:** Revenue shortfall

**Mitigation:**

- Aggressive marketing campaigns
- Promotional pricing
- User education
- QR quick login to reduce friction

### Risk 2: Price Sensitivity

**Impact:** Lower ARPU

**Mitigation:**

- Value-based pricing
- Package bundling
- Loyalty discounts
- Free trials

### Risk 3: Payment Failures

**Impact:** Lost revenue

**Mitigation:**

- Multiple payment methods
- Retry logic
- Clear error messages
- Support assistance

### Risk 4: Abuse/Fraud

**Impact:** Revenue leakage

**Mitigation:**

- Device binding
- Tethering detection
- Usage monitoring
- Automated enforcement
